Welcome to ChromaMusic Community Group

ChromaMusic Community Group was formed in 2021 with the aim of giving people the opportunity to come together to make music. The emphasis is to encourage people to participate, progress and perform.

Our aims

Play and make music freely, from those who have never played any instrument, sung or made music before, up to those who are seasoned professionals.

Participate in a creative community, collaborating to make music and connecting with others, whilst using music as a means to communicate.

Progress in their own personal development as they find their musical and creative voice.

Perform in appropriate safe settings to help develop confidence and empower people not just for music making but for other areas of their life.

ChromaMusic Community Training Orchestra every Monday - 7pm at St Catherine's Church hall, Gorseinon, Swansea, SA4 4US

Any orchestral instrument musicians Grade 3 and above are welcome!
