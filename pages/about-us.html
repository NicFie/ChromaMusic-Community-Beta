About us

ChromaMusic Community Group was founded by Alida Watters, a local piano, cello, and music theory teacher, who wanted to develop a musical community available for people of all ages and abilities.

Our training orchestra for those aged 14+ now comprises over 30 members of all ages and levels, from a wide area of South Wales. Some of us are learners, while others hadn’t picked up their instrument for over 40 years ​before joining - so don’t worry, there will be a place for you! We are a friendly and welcoming group, whose main aim is to enjoy the experience of learning to play together.

Hear some of our members talk about their experience in our training orchestra, and our inaugural concert celebrated on 19th March 2022, which was a huge success. Find out more here.

We have a wide range of instruments in our orchestra - violin, cello, double bass, tuba, flute and more! Please contact us if you would need to borrow a keyboard or music stand.

We are currently working on the BBC 40 Pieces. Each piece has three different arrangements which can be played simultaneously, so whatever your level, there will be an arrangement that will suit you.

Our next concert will be on October 22nd 2022. Tickets will be available closer to the date.
